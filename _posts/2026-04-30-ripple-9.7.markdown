The latest release of the Ripple UI firmwares has had quite a big overhaul (under the hood) and the UI now sports a Light and Dark mode theming, plus you can now customise the accent colour used throughout the firmware.

![](/assets/images/2026/04/30/t-pager-9.7.png)

## Display Preferences

The Display menu now shows these preferences:

<img alt class="mx-auto" src="/assets/images/2026/04/30/display-prefs-9.7.png" />

The **UI Theme** preferences is one of: Light, Dark or Auto. The Auto option automatically switches the UI theme depending on the **Brightness** setting (day or night).

PAID FEATURE: The **Accent color** lets you customise the accent colour used throughout the firmware. There are 10 fun colour swatches to select from:

<img alt class="mx-auto" src="/assets/images/2026/04/30/color-select.png" />

## Map Theme

Note that the **UI Theme** preference also determines the light/dark theming for the maps:

<img alt class="mx-auto" src="/assets/images/2026/04/30/map-dark-light.png" />
